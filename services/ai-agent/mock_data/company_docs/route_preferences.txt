LogiSecure Route Preferences — Operations Playbook

Preferred alternate ports (Europe):
  - Rotterdam disruption -> Antwerp (primary), Bremerhaven (overflow)
  - Hamburg disruption -> Rotterdam or Amsterdam
  - Amsterdam disruption -> Rotterdam or Zeebrugge

Preferred alternate routes (Asia-Pacific):
  - South China Sea storm -> hold Singapore or Hong Kong
  - Malacca Strait advisory -> widen transit corridor via Lombok Strait if escort delayed
  - Japan-Vancouver lane -> divert to Busan for hub transfer

Middle East / Africa:
  - Dubai congestion -> split at Jebel Ali or route via Salalah
  - Suez blockage -> Cape of Good Hope (mandatory for non-urgent sea freight)

Mode escalation matrix:
  - CRITICAL + medical/semiconductor → air charter eligible
  - HIGH + electronics → sea reroute first, air if delay > 72h
  - MEDIUM/LOW → land/sea only unless client opts in to expedite

Cost thresholds for auto-escalation to management:
  - Financial exposure > $1M USD
  - Delay > 96 hours on critical-priority manifest
