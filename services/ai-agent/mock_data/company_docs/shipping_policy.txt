LogiSecure Shipping Policy — Confidential (On-Prem Only)

1. Incident response SLA
   - HIGH/CRITICAL disruptions: notify affected clients within 2 hours.
   - MEDIUM disruptions: notify within 6 hours.
   - All notifications require human approval before send.

2. Port strike protocol
   - Divert Rotterdam/Hamburg/Amsterdam cargo to Antwerp or Bremerhaven when berth
     capacity allows.
   - Prioritize shipments marked "critical" or value > $500,000 USD.
   - Update fleet GPS and maritime AIS within 30 minutes of approved reroute.

3. Storm / weather protocol
   - Vessels in South China Sea or Pacific corridors must hold at nearest safe port
     if Beaufort scale exceeds 8.
   - Air-freight escalation allowed only for medical supplies and semiconductors.

4. Suez Canal blockage
   - Activate Cape of Good Hope alternate sea route.
   - Expect +10-14 day delay; pre-draft client communications with revised ETA.

5. Data confidentiality
   - All impact analysis and rerouting runs on-prem via AMD ROCm.
   - Zero cloud API calls for shipment manifests or client data.

6. Human-in-the-loop
   - No autonomous fleet dispatch without operations manager approval.
   - Client emails are drafts only until explicitly approved in the dashboard.
